Pool Location. 16th Street Park.

The Bayonne Municipal Pool is located in Bayonne, NJ. The pool is in the 16 Street Park by the Newark Bay.

The BMP serves Bayonne residents. Proof of residency is required at admission. Proof of residency is any official document with name and Bayonne address. If you're a Bayonne resident, you may apply for the Bayonne Residency ID card to come in with reduced rates.

G. Thomas Di Domenico Municipal Pool.

Dedicated, May 31, 1997.

This pool is dedicated to the Honorable G. Thomas Di Domenico. Di Domenico served as Bayonne's Mayor from 1955 - 1959. While in office, he took part in activities that have benefited all citizens of Bayonne. Thanks to his leadership and perseverance generations of boys and girls can enjoy his dream - The Bayonne Municipal Pool

Each season begins Memorial Day weekend, and open weekends only while schools are still in session. The pool is then open everyday the day after High School graduates. The pool remains open weather permitted until Labor Day.
